Summary of quality attributes for PolarSys projects

This document gathers the quality attributes computed on all analysed projects, summarises them, and proposes a transverse perspective on the projects ratings. Its main purpose is to help users understand the differences between projects and get hints as how to improve their practices.

This is a dynamically generated document: it takes as input the attributes.csv file generated by the dashboard, then outputs a summary and plots the values for all projects. In this very case, 11 projects with a total of 18 quality attributes have been analysed.

Quick Summary

Main quality characteristics

Maturity

The overall rating of a project is defined as its maturity. It is defined as the mean of its subcharacteristics: Ecosystem, Process, Product, and reflects the conformance of the project to the set of quality requirements established for the measurement process.

Ecosystem

The degree of maturity for the ecosystem evolving around the project: is it active, diversified, responsive?
